Current Research

I am presently working with Professor Xin Yong's SaIL lab at the University at Buffalo to spearhead the development of simulations investigating nanoparticle transport within polymer networks under a periodic shear, aiming to better understand interactions that play a vital role in enhanced drug delivery, filtration systems, and a myriad of other areas that impact our daily lives. I primarily work with the Python programming language and open-source software such as LAMMPS and OVITO. My work will be used to complement and validate experimental results to achieve a comprehensive characterization of the effect of shear on nanoparticle diffusion.
